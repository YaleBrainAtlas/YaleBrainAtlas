Features

Atlas
Use the Atlas Viewer to inspect the 696 parcels of the Yale Brain Atlas.
Multimodal Database
Explore multimodal data localized to each parcel of the Yale Brain Atlas. Modalities include iEEG, PET, fMRI, white matter connectivity, stimulation mapping, among others.
Educational Resources
Access robust educational resources. The intuitive parcel nomenclature of the Yale Brain Atlas makes it ideal for learning neuroanatomy.
