Below are the publications detailing the creation and applications of Yale Brain Atlas (YBA). More publications coming soon!

Creating YBA
McGrath et al. outlines creating the parcellation and nomenclature of YBA.

Scientific Reports (2022)
Using YBA to map structure-function relationships
Collins et al. describes how structure-function coupling varies by region and function in the brain using YBA.

Nature Communications (2024)
Using YBA to localize stimulation sites for identifying seizure onset zones
Sivaraju et al. localizes 1 Hz direct electrical stimulation sites in YBA to identify seizure onset zones.

Brain Stimulation (2024)
Creating interactive tools and data for YBA
Collins et al. develops interactive tools (as featured on this website) and open-sources neuroimaging data for YBA.
